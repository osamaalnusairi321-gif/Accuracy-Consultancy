Who we are

Accuracy Consultancy & Research Firm was founded in 2005 on the belief that better decisions befin with reliable evidence. Since then, we have supported governments, United Nations agencies, international organisations, and private companies through research, M&E, strategy organisational development, and practical advisory work.

Our experience was built in Yemen, where complex operating conditions demanded careful planning, strong field access, and the ability to adapt quickly. Today, we support projects in Yemen, Saudi Arabia, and the United States, with services that include data analysis, didgital transformation, dashboards, GIS, and decision-support solutions

With a network of more that 600 field professionals, over 100 completed assignements, and more than 20 international clients, Accuracy brings together local reach and technical expertise. We do more than produce reports; we work with clients to strengthen systems, improve performance, and turn plans into measurable results.

What We Stand For

Our values

01

Integrity

We maintain full independence and transparency in every engagement. Our findings are reported as they are; not shaped by what any party hopes to hear. This commitment to honest analysis is the foundation of the trust our clients place in us.

02

Precision

We hold our work to the highest analytical and methodological standards. Whether designing a data collection tool or drafting a final report, we apply the same discipline: clear thinking, careful language, and conclusions that evidence can actually support.

03

Relevance

We design for context. Our approach is shaped by the realities of the environments we operate in — not by templates borrowed from more stable settings. Contextual intelligence is not a bonus; it is a requirement of quality work in complex environments.

04

Partnership

We treat every client relationship as a genuine collaboration. Our goal is not to deliver a report and move on, but to contribute to outcomes that matter — working alongside our clients to ensure that findings translate into action.

How We Work

Our approach to every engagement

Our approach is built on a foundational premise: credible information leads to better decisions. Whether we are designing a monitoring framework, delivering a training programme, or providing strategic advice, our process is driven by evidence, informed by context, and oriented toward practical outcomes.

We do not apply one-size-fits-all methodologies. Each engagement begins with a careful assessment of the operating environment, the client's objectives, and the specific challenges to be addressed. From there, we build tailored frameworks that can withstand scrutiny and deliver the information stakeholders actually need.

Throughout every engagement, we maintain open communication with our clients — sharing early findings, flagging risks, and adapting as circumstances evolve. We believe that the best consulting relationships are collaborative ones.

1

Context Assessment

We begin with a thorough understanding of the operating environment, client needs, and the constraints shaping the engagement.

2

Framework Design

We develop bespoke methodological frameworks — monitoring systems, training curricula, evaluation tools — tailored to what the context demands.

3

Field Implementation

We execute with discipline and adaptability — maintaining methodological rigour while remaining responsive to on-the-ground realities.

4

Analysis & Reporting

We synthesise findings with precision, presenting clear, evidence-based conclusions and actionable recommendations that stakeholders can act on.

5

Follow-Through

Where appropriate, we support clients in translating findings and recommendations into concrete organisational or programmatic change.
